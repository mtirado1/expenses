
Example expenses file

2026-05-10    $100     Sold old sofa
Lines that don't match the expense regexp will be ignored.

Expenses can be entered as markdown lists
- 2026-10-25 -$57.50   Halloween costume
- 2026-12-10 -$223.34  Christmas gifts

Multiple tags are allowed, the first one is the category:
- 2026-12-15  +$800    #job Pay day
- 2026-08-10  -$500    #car #repair Fixed the engine
